Why choose cars from the USA?

We compared the US markets with European ones and realized that buying a car in America is several times more profitable, no matter how paradoxical it may sound. This is due to thoughtful logistics, the level of development of services for assessing the condition of a car and the very process of buying a car.

Most US citizens lease a car for several years and during the entire operation the leasing company itself is engaged in constant maintenance of the car, as a result of which cars from the USA are one of the best choices for motorists in Moldova.

Why it is so cheap?

Wrecked cars from the United States are bought out at auctions by insurance companies. At these auctions, the car loses half the price even due to minimal damage. If we take into account the monetary costs, namely, redemption, delivery, customs and repair, then the price of a car similar to the condition in Moldova will be 35-50% higher, and new ones will cost space money.
